NOTE => without app.use() you can't use an Application Level Middleware OR Route Level Middleware in ExpressJS

In Application Level Middleware first  you have to involve an app.use and after another some routes like  '/' '/about' etc...  without app.use() they not work...


In Route Level Middleware first  you have to involve an app.use and after another some routes like  '/' '/about' etc in Router.get etc... without app.use() they not work...
